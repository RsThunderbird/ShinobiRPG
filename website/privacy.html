The Hidden Village Records (Privacy Policy)

Last Updated: September 28, 2025

BRIEF: This document outlines what information Shinobi RPG collects, why it is collected, and how it is used. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Scrolls of Identity: Data Collection

The Bot collects the minimum necessary information to operate the game and maintain your progress:

1.1 Discord Data

  • Discord User ID (UID): Your unique identifier used to link game stats.
  • Guild (Server) ID: Used for per-server configuration.

1.2 Gameplay Data

  • In-Game Progress: Level, currency, inventory, and mission status.
  • Command Timestamps: Used for rate-limiting and anti-abuse.

2. Use of Collected Data

Your data is used exclusively for the game's functionality, technical support, and leaderboards. We do not sell or trade your data.

3. Third-Party Intelligence (AI)

Optional AI features are powered by Google's Gemini. Prompts are sent to Google's API but are not permanently stored by us. Google's own privacy policies apply to that processing.

4. Data Security

All gameplay data is stored in a private database with restricted access. While we use industry-standard measures, no method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights: Data Control

You have the right to request deletion of your data. Contact us via our official Discord to request a full wipe of your game profile. Note: This action is permanent and irreversible.

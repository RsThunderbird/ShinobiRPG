## Starting Jutsus

### Attack
**Chakra Cost:** 0  
**How to Get:** Starting Jutsu  
**What it Does:** Your basic attack. A fundamental technique every shinobi starts with, dealing damage based on your power stat.

### Transformation Jutsu
**Chakra Cost:** 5  
**How to Get:** Starting Jutsu  
**What it Does:** Transform, gaining a massive power boost for 3 rounds. Your power multiplies by 5x, making this a solid early-game buff.

### Infused Chakra Blade
**Chakra Cost:** 6  
**How to Get:** Obtained during tutorial  
**What it Does:** Strike with chakra-infused blades that cause bleeding and massively increase your power. Deals solid damage, applies a 15% bleed effect for 1 round, and boosts your power by 25x for 2 rounds.

## Genin Rank Jutsus

### Mystic Palm
**Chakra Cost:** 4  
**How to Get:** Obtained from Sakura mentor  
**What it Does:** A healing technique that restores health based on your missing HP. The lower your health, the more effective this becomes. Heals 40% of your max health plus a flat 100 HP.

### Summon Ninken
**Chakra Cost:** 11  
**How to Get:** Obtained from Kakashi mentor  
**What it Does:** Summon ninja dogs to pin down your opponent, stunning them for 2 rounds. Perfect for setting up devastating combos.

### Analysis
**Chakra Cost:** 0  
**How to Get:** Obtained from Shikamaru mentor  
**What it Does:** Take a moment to strategize and regain chakra. Grants 5 chakra instantly with no cost - essentially a free chakra refill.

### Fireball Jutsu
**Chakra Cost:** 7  
**How to Get:** Obtained from Sasuke mentor  
**What it Does:** Launch a massive fireball at your enemy. A classic Uchiha technique that deals respectable fire damage.

### Shadow Clone Jutsu
**Chakra Cost:** 5  
**How to Get:** Obtained from Naruto mentor  
**What it Does:** Summon clones to overwhelm your opponent with numbers. Your clones attack together, dealing heavy damage in a coordinated assault.

### One Thousand Years of Death
**Chakra Cost:** 1  
**How to Get:** Obtained during Kakashi Hokage trials  
**What it Does:** The legendary technique... you poke the enemy's butt. Surprisingly effective for its low cost, though the damage is minimal.

### Burning Ash
**Chakra Cost:** 5  
**How to Get:** Obtained during Hiruzen Hokage trials  
**What it Does:** Spew ash across the battlefield that ignites, burning your opponent. Deals damage and applies a burn effect that chips away 10% of their health per round for 2 rounds.

## Akatsuki Event Exclusive

### Izanami
**Chakra Cost:** 0  
**How to Get:** Akatsuki Event (Currently unobtainable)  
**What it Does:** A forbidden technique that controls space and time. Sets your HP and Chakra to infinity for 3 rounds (Recording Phase). ## üéØ Pro Tips

1. **Combo Potential**: Jutsus that stun (like Summon Ninken, Shadow Strangle) are perfect for setting up high-damage attacks.

2. **Chakra Management**: Don't spam expensive jutsus. Use Analysis or other chakra-gain techniques to maintain your reserves.

3. **Status Effects Stack**: Bleeding, burning, and poison can stack up quickly. Use multiple status-applying jutsus for devastating damage over time.

4. **Defense Breakers**: Jutsus like Guillotine Drop and Crystal Palace that reduce or ignore defense are crucial against tanky opponents.

5. **Healing Timing**: Save healing jutsus like Mystic Palm Enhanced for when you're below 50% health for maximum efficiency.

6. **Event Exclusives**: Keep an eye out for limited-time events - they often have the most powerful jutsus.
